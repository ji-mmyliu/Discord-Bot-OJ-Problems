```
For this problem, you will be given a string. You will be given a task
to perform on this string. The task consists of two integers: L and R. For
the task, you are to print the substring characters from L to R (including both L and R).
If, however, either L or R are out of bounds, or a substring from L to R cannot be
made, print "none".

Input specification:
First line: The string
Second line: Two numbers: L, then R (seperated by spaces).


Sample input 1:
abcdefg
1 3

Sample output 1:
abc


Sample input 2:
school
3 1

Sample output 2:
none
```
